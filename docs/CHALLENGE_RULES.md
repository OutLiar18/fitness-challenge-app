# 🏆 Champions Legacy Challenge

# Challenge Rules

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official rules of the Champions Legacy Challenge.

It is intended to be the single source of truth for:

- Participants
- Administrators
- Future development

Application code should follow these rules wherever practical.

---

# Challenge Philosophy

Champions Legacy exists to encourage long-term personal growth.

The challenge rewards:

- Consistency
- Discipline
- Honesty
- Healthy habits
- Continuous learning

The objective is not perfection.

The objective is becoming better than yesterday.

---

# General Rules

Participants may complete activities in any order.

Activities may be completed throughout the day.

Entries should be submitted honestly.

Where proof is optional, honesty is expected.

Administrators reserve the right to investigate suspicious entries.

# Water

Daily Goal

2000 ml

Examples

- Water
- Sparkling water

Does NOT count

- Coffee
- Tea
- Soft drinks
- Juice
- Energy drinks

Rule

Only plain drinking water counts.

# Fruit

Daily Goal

3 servings

Examples

- Apple
- Banana
- Orange
- Pear
- Mango
- Grapes
- Pineapple

Does NOT count

- Tomatoes
- Avocados
- Cucumbers

Rule

The challenge follows the culinary definition of fruit rather than the botanical definition.

Maximum Daily Progress

3 servings.

Extra fruit is encouraged but does not increase challenge completion.

# Daily Completion

A category is considered complete when its daily goal has been reached.

Progress beyond the goal may still be recorded for statistics.

Example

Water Goal

2000 ml

Recorded

2600 ml

Daily Goal

✅ Complete

Statistics

2600 ml stored

---

# Workout Rules

Workout categories measure repetitions.

Not exercises.

Example

20 Push-ups

15 Pull-ups

15 Dips

=

50 Upper Body reps

Challenge Complete

This prevents users from being forced to perform only one exercise.

---

# Honesty Policy

Champions Legacy is built on trust.

Participants are expected to record truthful entries.

The challenge rewards integrity over competition.

---

# Fair Play

Participants should not:

- Submit fake entries
- Submit duplicate entries intentionally
- Manipulate statistics
- Exploit bugs

Future versions may include anti-cheat systems.

---

# Future Scoring

Points will eventually reward:

Daily completion

Consistency

Streaks

Achievements

Milestones

Bonus challenges

Statistics

Entries themselves will never permanently store calculated points.

---

# Streak Rules (Future)

A streak increases when at least one challenge category is completed during a day.

Missing a day resets the streak.

Grace mechanics may be introduced in future versions.

---

# Administrator Guidelines

Administrators should encourage participants.

Corrections should be educational rather than punitive.

Whenever possible, automation should replace manual work.

---

# Future Rule Changes

Challenge rules may evolve.

When rules change:

Historical entries remain valid.

Statistics should adapt automatically wherever possible.
