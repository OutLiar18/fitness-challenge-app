# Champions Legacy

# Experimental Ideas

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

Experimental Ideas are concepts that appear promising but require testing before becoming official features.

These ideas should never be considered permanent parts of Champions Legacy until proven successful.

---

# Philosophy

Innovation requires experimentation.

Not every experiment succeeds.

That is expected.

---

# Examples

Examples of experimental ideas include:

- Alternative scoring systems
- New progression models
- AI coaching concepts
- Dynamic challenges
- Social experiments
- New competition formats

---

# Information to Record

Each experiment should include:

- Name
- Description
- Hypothesis
- Expected Benefits
- Potential Risks
- Success Criteria
- Outcome

---

# Possible Outcomes

Successful

Needs Revision

Rejected

Deferred

---

# Guiding Principle

Experiment boldly.

Keep only what genuinely improves the platform.
