# Idea Inbox

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

The Idea Inbox is the temporary home for every new idea related to Champions Legacy.

Ideas placed here have not yet been evaluated.

They may eventually become planned features, experimental concepts or be rejected entirely.

The purpose of this document is to capture inspiration before it is forgotten.

---

# Philosophy

Every idea deserves to be recorded.

Not every idea deserves to be built.

Ideas should be written quickly without worrying about implementation details.

Evaluation happens later.

---

# What Belongs Here?

Examples include:

- New challenge categories
- Game mechanics
- Reward ideas
- League features
- User interface ideas
- Social features
- AI concepts
- Quality of life improvements
- Seasonal events
- Community suggestions

---

# Information to Record

Each idea should include:

## Title

A short descriptive name.

---

## Summary

A brief explanation of the idea.

---

## Why?

What problem does it solve?

What value does it provide?

---

## Initial Thoughts

Early considerations, concerns or possible improvements.

---

## Status

Every idea should begin as:

Inbox

---

# Evaluation Process

Ideas should eventually be moved to one of the following locations:

- Planned Ideas
- Experimental Ideas
- Rejected or Deferred

The inbox should not become permanent storage.

---

# Guiding Principle

Capture first.

Evaluate later.
