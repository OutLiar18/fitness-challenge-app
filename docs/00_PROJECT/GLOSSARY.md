# Champions Legacy

# Glossary

---

# Purpose

This glossary defines the official terminology used throughout Champions Legacy.

These definitions ensure consistency across documentation, development and future conversations.

Where possible, every important term should have one agreed meaning.

---

# Activity

A measurable action performed by a user that contributes towards personal growth.

Examples include:

- Drinking water
- Reading
- Running
- Completing a workout
- Skill practice

Activities form the foundation of progression.

---

# Achievement

A permanent reward earned by completing specific milestones or challenges.

Achievements recognise meaningful accomplishments rather than daily actions.

---

# Admin

A user responsible for configuring and managing leagues, challenges and platform settings.

Administrators oversee competitions but participate under the same rules as other users unless otherwise specified.

---

# Category

A type of activity tracked by Champions Legacy.

Examples:

- Water
- Reading
- Running
- Upper Body
- Core
- Skill Development

Categories define what users can record.

---

# Challenge

A structured event where participants complete activities according to defined rules.

Challenges may be personal, team-based or community-wide.

Multiple challenges may exist within Champions Legacy.

---

# Champion

A participant who consistently strives to become better than yesterday.

Being a Champion is defined by attitude and commitment rather than leaderboard position.

---

# Consistency

The repeated performance of positive actions over time.

Consistency is valued more highly than isolated exceptional performances.

---

# Effective Reps

A normalised measurement used to estimate meaningful training effort across different exercises.

Effective Reps provide a fairer comparison between exercises with different levels of difficulty.

---

# Entry

A single recorded submission made by a user.

Each entry belongs to a category and contributes towards progression.

---

# Experience (XP)

The primary progression currency earned by completing meaningful activities.

XP represents personal growth rather than skill level.

---

# Growth

Positive personal development achieved through consistent action.

Growth may be physical, mental, educational or behavioural.

---

# Journal

A personal record allowing users to reflect on their daily progress.

Journal entries provide context beyond numerical statistics.

---

# League

A seasonal competitive environment where participants earn points, levels and rankings.

League progress resets between seasons while Personal Mode continues permanently.

---

# Legacy

The long-term result of thousands of consistent positive actions.

Within Champions Legacy, a legacy is built gradually through everyday decisions.

---

# Level

A representation of accumulated long-term progress.

Levels provide recognition without defining a person's worth.

---

# Library

A reusable collection of user-created items.

Examples include:

- Books
- Exercises
- Meals (future)

Libraries reduce repetitive data entry.

---

# Milestone

A significant point in a user's journey.

Milestones often unlock achievements, titles or recognition.

---

# Mission

The practical purpose that guides the day-to-day development of Champions Legacy.

---

# Personal Best

The highest verified performance achieved by an individual in a measurable activity.

---

# Personal Mode

The permanent progression system that never resets.

Personal Mode tracks lifelong growth regardless of seasonal competitions.

---

# Pillar

One of the six major areas that define the structure of Champions Legacy.

Current pillars are:

- Growth
- Motivation
- Competition
- Reflection
- Community
- Intelligence

---

# Platform

Champions Legacy itself.

The platform contains multiple systems, modes and challenges.

---

# Points

The competitive scoring currency used within challenges and leagues.

Points measure participation and contribution during a specific competition.

Unlike XP, points are typically seasonal.

---

# Progress

Meaningful improvement achieved through consistent effort over time.

Progress is measured against previous versions of oneself rather than other people.

---

# Reflection

The process of reviewing personal progress to recognise improvement, identify lessons and strengthen motivation.

---

# Season

A fixed competitive period within League Mode.

Each season has its own rankings, awards and results.

---

# Streak

A consecutive series of successful days meeting defined criteria.

Streaks exist to encourage consistency rather than perfection.

---

# Team

A group of participants working together within a challenge or league.

Teams encourage accountability and shared success.

---

# Timeline

A chronological history of a user's journey.

The timeline allows users to revisit milestones, achievements and meaningful moments.

---

# Title

A cosmetic recognition earned through long-term progression or significant accomplishments.

Titles do not affect gameplay or competition.

---

# User

Any individual using Champions Legacy.

Depending on permissions, users may be participants, administrators or future moderation roles.

---

# Vision

The long-term aspiration that defines what Champions Legacy ultimately seeks to become.

---

# XP

See **Experience (XP)**.

---

# Better Than Yesterday

The core philosophy of Champions Legacy.

Users are encouraged to compare themselves only with who they were yesterday.

This principle underpins every feature, system and design decision throughout the platform.
